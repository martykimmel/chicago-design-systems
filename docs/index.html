Fall 2019

Northern Trust

How does a 130-year-old bank create, support and scale a design system?

Members of Northern Trust's product design and UI services team will provide a look into their multi-year journey to build a design system for their client-facing digital experiences. You'll learn about the ecosystem of visual and technical resources and tooling they use today, as well as how they grew support and use of the system through educational resources and more.

The team will cover:

  • Scaling a design system with limited resources
  • Design tooling and multi-disciplinary collaboration
  • Technical delivery and approach

Spring 2019

Conversant

Conversant is a leader in interaction management, empowering brands to transform ordinary customer experiences into meaningful, human experiences. Their connected suite of personalized digital media products and services combines leading-edge identity management, data science expertise and privacy by design to create human connections that count, everywhere. As part of Epsilon, Conversant employs more than 2,100 associates in 17 offices worldwide.

Learn about the unique ways Conversant built their enterprise design system as well as the different approach they’ve taken to establishing UX as a practice in their organization. The tools that support Conversant’s business both internally and externally are extremely diverse and complex. Maintaining a design system flexible enough to handle those business needs requires ongoing strategic alignment, forward-thinking UX and UI, and smart, efficient code. In this meetup, we’ll do a deep dive into what makes Conversant’s design system so successful and cover tactical ways to solving enterprise-level challenges.

Before the Design System… Research!

Methods, audits and communications that can help shape the design system before any documentation or code is written. What do your users need, and what will be the most beneficial for your specific use-case?

Winter 2018

Morningstar Design System: A Reflection On the Value We’ve Delivered In Our First Year

Morningstar’s Design System team will join us on Thursday, January 18th to share their journey from several disparate design systems toward a centralized team driving change across the organization. We will learn how Morningstar defined the need for a design system internally and how they delivered on their strategy. The talk will cover:

  • The value that the design system has already brought Morningstar
  • How it has strengthened Morningstar's visual style and UI components across product teams
  • How the team collaborates and drives change across the organization
  • How product teams are adopting the design system
